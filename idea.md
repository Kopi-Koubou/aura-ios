# Horoscope/MBTI App

## Vision

A delightful daily horoscope and MBTI personality insights app. Users get personalized daily horoscopes based on their zodiac sign, plus MBTI-based personality analysis and compatibility features.

## Core Value Proposition

- Daily personalized horoscope readings
- MBTI personality test and detailed profiles
- Zodiac + MBTI combined insights
- Freemium model: free daily horoscopes, premium for advanced features

## Target Users

- Young adults interested in astrology and personality types
- People who check horoscopes daily
- MBTI enthusiasts wanting deeper insights

## Success Metrics

- Daily active users (DAU)
- Free-to-paid conversion rate
- Retention (D7, D30)
- App store rating

## Sprint Alignment

Directly supports 60-day sprint goal: **Horoscope/MBTI app live by Apr 6**

Revenue target: $5K/month via freemium subscriptions
